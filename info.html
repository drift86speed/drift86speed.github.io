Drift Points

As you probably know that drift points are gotten by doing a drift.
The longer you drift the more points you get and you get a higher multiplier.
If you have some drift points and you fail to do a drift before the blue bar behind the score is gone you get the points from your drifts but you lose your multiplier.
In multiplayer the way DP works is different, in multiplayer you get more points but your DP combo breaks faster when not drifting
(If you go sideways in the air it counts as drifting and will give you more points this can most clearly be seen in Port when going fast enough over the bridge)
